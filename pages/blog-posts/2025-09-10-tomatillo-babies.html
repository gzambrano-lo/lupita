my tomatillo babies

posted sep 10, 2025

today i saw the first green

tiny sprouts poking up through the soil

it feels like magic every time.

a dry little seed turning into life

the purple and the green are side by side.

leaning into the light

they look delicate now

but i know they'll grow strong if i keep caring for them

watering, waiting. checking on them each day

already feels like a little ritual

these seedlings make me smile

they remind that growth is slow

but its happening, even when i cant see it yet

a good start.

a good day.
