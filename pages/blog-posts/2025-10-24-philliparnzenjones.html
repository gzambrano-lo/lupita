artist spotlight: philip arnzen-jones

posted nov 24, 2025

i don't know how else to say this but this piece by philip arnzen-jones hits every neuron in my brain at once.

it's got:

— squidbillies
— the “i don't netflix & chill, i vhs and fuck” trucker hat
— the bottle of moonshine just vibing
— the iconic shotgun. can't forget it
— the porch that looks like it survived 17 divorces
— pastels tying all of this chaos together for no reason

this FUCKSSSSS.

it's brilliant, it's grimy, it's tender, it's exactly my type of unhinged comfort art. philip arnzen-jones is sick asf.

you can follow him here. he deserves ALL the hype: @arnzenjonesart
