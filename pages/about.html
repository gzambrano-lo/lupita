hi, i'm lupe ✨

i make little websites, play with color palettes, and collect half-finished ideas like they're postcards. i like building soft corners of the web... quiet, nostalgic spaces that feel more human than polished.

most of what i do here is just tinkering and documenting. a mix of experiments, notes, and things that make me happy. i treat this site like a digital sketchbook. part learning log, part comfort project.

español

hago sitios pequeños, juego con colores y guardo ideas a medio terminar como si fueran postales. me gusta construir rincones suaves del internet: espacios tranquilos y un poco nostálgicos, más humanos que perfectos.

casi todo lo que hago aquí es experimentar y dejar registro. una mezcla de cosas que aprendo, notas, y cosas que me hacen feliz. trato esta pagina web como un cuaderno digital. un lugar para aprender, probar y soltar lo que llevo en la cabeza.

what i'm learning

i'm teaching myself front-end development: html, css, and accessibility and slowly working through javascript and python basics. i like understanding how things work under the hood, even if i break them a few times first.

my goal is to keep creating small, intentional web spaces that feel personal, kind, and maybe a little weird.

story (short)

the web has always felt like home to me: messy, creative, full of people building things for the love of it. this site is a continuation of that spirit.

full story (content note)

in 2019 i hit a hard reset. therapy, community, and small creative projects pulled me back to myself. i started learning tech as a way to rebuild, something i could shape, fix, and understand. that turned into learning web development and sharing what i make here, quietly and at my own pace.
